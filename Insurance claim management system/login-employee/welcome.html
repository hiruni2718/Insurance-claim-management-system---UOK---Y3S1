Services

In today's fast-paced digital world, we understand the importance of convenience and efficiency when it comes to managing your insurance claims. That's why we are thrilled to introduce our online claim registration and settlement services. With our user-friendly online platform, we aim to simplify the entire claims process, from registration to settlement, providing you with a seamless and hassle-free experience.

Online Claim Registration

Our online claim registration feature allows you to initiate your claim from the comfort of your own home or office. No more waiting in long queues or spending hours on the phone. By logging into your account on our secure platform, you can easily register your claim by providing the necessary details and uploading any supporting documents. This streamlined process eliminates paperwork and reduces processing time, ensuring that your claim is initiated swiftly.

Online Claim Settlement

Our online claim settlement service revolutionizes the way you receive your insurance benefits. With our digital platform, you can track the progress of your claim and receive settlement updates in real-time. Once your claim is approved, the settlement amount will be transferred directly to your designated bank account, ensuring a secure and swift transaction. Our online claim settlement offers a range of benefits to ensure a smooth and efficient experience. Our online claim settlement offers speedy processing, transparency, and secure transactions.

Claim details update

Our online customer claim details update feature allows you to conveniently and securely update important information related to your claim. With just a few clicks, you can provide us with any necessary updates or additional details regarding your claim, ensuring that we have the most accurate and up-to-date information to process your claim effectively. This streamlined process saves you time and eliminates the need for manual paperwork or phone calls, enabling us to serve you better and expedite the resolution of your claim.

About

We are Sri Lanka Insurance Corporation, the largest state-owned insurer in Sri Lanka anchored to a pioneering legacy of financial stability and trust spanning over five decades. Established in 1962 as a state owned corporation and the pioneer insurer in the country , Sri Lanka Insurance today manages an asset base of over LKR 268 billion which is the largest in the industry, the largest life insurance fund in the local insurance industry amounting in excess of LKR 152.5 billion, and a LKR 6 billion strong capitalization, making us one of the most secure and reliable insurance solutions providers in the country.

Our reputation of trust is founded upon a highly skilled and seasoned team of insurance experts manning a network of 158 branches across Sri Lanka, utilizing a rich and perfected technical knowledge base that is unrivaled to date in Sri Lanka. Over the decades, we have progressed from strength to strength with diversified interests and investments across many key industries such as banking & finance, healthcare, power & energy, travel & leisure as well as construction & engineering.
